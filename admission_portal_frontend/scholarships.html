General Guidelines 2019/2020 Academic Year
Guidelines
  • Please complete this application with your parents or legal guardians and return it along with your admissions application form. You must submit BOTH forms at the same time
  • Be sure to include supporting documentation for your scholarship application (e.g. loan documents, bank statements, and pay slips) for at least the last three (3) months.
  • Ashesi has limited resources and therefore cannot provide nancial assistance to all applicants. We cannot guarantee you will receive the full amount requested so it is critical that you do not request more assistance than you need. Scholarships are limited and only for students who cannot aord to pay the full fees.
  • Include a typed letter detailing the reasons why you need a scholarship from Ashesi University.
  • All the information provided must be accurate and true. Any falsication of information on this application may result in the withdrawal of your admission to the University
